# intake.md — what Visit Ready asks for

Visit Ready needs only a handful of facts to build a useful sheet. It asks for what's missing, in one short batch — never an interrogation.

## Minimum to start
- **What's going on**, in your own words.
- **Which specialist or doctor** the visit is with (or "not sure yet").

## Asked only if missing
- **When it started**, and whether it's getting better, worse, or staying steady.
- **Your current medications and supplements** (including over-the-counter and vitamins).
- **The one thing you most want to walk out knowing.**

## Never required
- A self-diagnosis. If you offer one, it's recorded as "patient's own words," never adopted as fact. (This is also why a condition you name as your own history is fine — it's *your* word, not the tool's verdict. See [`INVARIANT.md`](INVARIANT.md).)
- Anything you'd rather not share. You decide what goes on the sheet and what you say in the room.

If a fact is unavailable, Visit Ready records it as "to confirm with the doctor" rather than stalling or guessing.
